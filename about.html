About Industrial Power Solutions

Three Decades of Industrial Excellence & Reliability

Our Story

Founded in 1993

Industrial Power Solutions was established with a singular mission: to deliver the most reliable and cost-effective transformer solutions to industrial operations worldwide.

What started as a small manufacturing operation has grown into a trusted name recognized across multiple continents. Our growth is built on the foundation of unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Today, we operate state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and maintain regional service centers to ensure optimal support for our customers.

Our Evolution

1993: Company founded with focus on power transformer manufacturing

1998: Expanded to distribution transformers and power house solutions

2005: Achieved ISO 9001 certification for quality management

2012: Opened three new regional service centers

2018: Introduced advanced testing facilities and digital service platforms

2024: Serving 500+ industrial clients across 40+ countries
